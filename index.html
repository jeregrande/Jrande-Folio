Who I am

Software developer based in CA

Originally from the Sacramento region in California, I initially had an inclination towards all things mechanical and had gotten involved with shop classes since middle school. Having taught as a TA for an engineering course focusing on drafting and CNC equipment operations, it wasn't until my senior year in high school that I discovered my passion for software engineering.

Since then, I have graduated from San Jose State University with a Bachelors of Science in Software Engineering, having gained a techincal baseline of knowledge in OOD, SDLC, and full-stack web development. I now primarily use JS & Python to realize my passion for both building progressive web applications and exploring machine learning.
