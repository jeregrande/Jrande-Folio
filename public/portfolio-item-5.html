A lite screen recorder built with Electron's desktopCapturer and packaged with Electron Forge. Capture any window and save it to your desktop as a .webm video with this simple tool.

A recreational project I built with the intent of grasping the native desktop application stack with Electron as well as to understand Electron's built-in services.

It boasts a sleek, easy-to-use interface to stay pleasing to the eyes while providing a rich user experience. Simply choose your video source and press Start to begin recording.
